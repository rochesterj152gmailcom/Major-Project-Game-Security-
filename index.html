Core Awareness Topics

Common Cyber Security Threats

These are some of the most common online dangers affecting gamers. The goal of this website is to help users recognize these threats early and respond in safer ways.

🎣

Phishing Scams

Fake login pages, fake giveaways, suspicious reward messages, and scam emails designed to steal account credentials.

🔐

Weak Passwords

Reused or simple passwords make it much easier for attackers to gain access to gaming, email, or social accounts.

🦠

Malicious Downloads

Unofficial mods, cheats, cracked files, and fake installers can contain malware, spyware, or credential stealers.

📱

Unsafe Mobile Apps

Unverified gaming and betting apps can expose users to privacy risks, fake transactions, and poor security practices.

🌐

Suspicious Links

Links shared in chats, DMs, ads, or fake support pages may redirect users to dangerous websites or credential traps.

👤

Account Takeovers

Once attackers get login details, they may lock users out, steal purchases, impersonate them, or misuse saved payment data.
